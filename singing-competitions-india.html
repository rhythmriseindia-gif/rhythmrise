Singing Competitions in India

India has always been a land of rich musical heritage, nurturing voices across classical, folk, semi-classical, and contemporary genres. Rhythm Rise proudly presents one of the most professional singing competitions in India, designed to discover, encourage, and elevate genuine vocal talent.

About the Competition

Rhythm Rise singing competitions are open to participants across India, offering a transparent judging process led by international judges from India, Qatar, UK, Ireland, and USA. Our platform focuses on voice quality, sur (pitch), expression, stage presence, and musicality.

Rhythm Rise is a trusted platform for professional singing competitions in India. Learn more about our journey on the Rhythm Rise home page or complete your participation through our official registration page.
