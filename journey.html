The Journey

Rhythm Rise is built on years of cultural leadership, community engagement, and successful talent platforms across Doha and beyond.

Doha Music Lovers

Cultural & Music Community Platform – Doha, Qatar

Founded by Syed Rafi, Doha Music Lovers became a respected stage for emerging artists, encouraging multicultural participation and high-quality performances within the community.

Super Dancer (Season 1 – 3)

Flagship Dance Competition – Doha

A large-scale competitive platform that showcased exceptional dance talent across multiple seasons, judged by experienced professionals and supported by strong audience engagement.

Rhythm Rise

International Singing & Dance Talent Platform

Building on years of experience, Rhythm Rise was launched as a global platform bringing together singers and dancers with international jury members, transparent judging, and professional exposure.
