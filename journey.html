The Journey

Rhythm Rise is built on years of cultural leadership, community engagement, and successful large-scale talent platforms across Doha and online.

Doha Music Lovers

Cultural & Music Community Platform — Doha, Qatar

Founded by Syed Rafi, Doha Music Lovers emerged as a vibrant cultural platform dedicated to nurturing upcoming artists and promoting artistic collaboration within the expatriate community of Qatar.

The initiative provided a respected stage for singers and performers from diverse backgrounds, including artists from Bangladesh, Nepal, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka, encouraging multicultural participation and unity.

Doha Music Lovers laid the foundation for several large-scale competitions and directly shaped the vision behind Rhythm Rise.

Super Dancer — Doha, Qatar

Season 1 | Flagship Dance Competition

Super Dancer Season 1 marked the launch of a professional dance competition platform in Doha, featuring classical, contemporary, and fusion dance styles judged by experienced experts.

Super Dancer — Season 2

Expanded Scale & Higher Standards

Season 2 elevated the competition with increased participation, improved production quality, and refined judging parameters, strengthening its reputation across Doha’s dance community.

Super Dancer — Season 3

Established & Recognized Platform

Season 3 reinforced Super Dancer as one of Doha’s most credible dance competitions, with strong audience engagement and continued artistic excellence.

Super Singer — Doha, Qatar

Vocal Talent Platform

Super Singer provided a structured and transparent platform for vocalists, focusing on musicality, voice control, and performance quality.

Voice of World

Global Online Singing Contest

Voice of World connected singers globally through an online-first format, showcasing talent beyond geographical boundaries.

Cric Qatar

Community Cricket Platform

Cric Qatar is a leading community cricket platform supporting structured tournaments, team engagement, and sporting excellence across Qatar.
