The Journey

Rhythm Rise stands on a strong foundation of successful cultural, musical, and sporting initiatives conducted across Doha and online platforms. Each program represents our commitment to quality, inclusivity, and global community engagement.

Super Dancer — Doha, Qatar

Flagship Dance Competition

Super Dancer marked the beginning of a large-scale dance platform in Doha, bringing together performers across age groups and styles. The event celebrated classical, contemporary, and fusion dance forms, judged by experienced local and international experts.

Super Dancer – Season 2

Expanded Participation & Higher Standards

Season 2 raised the bar with enhanced production quality, refined judging parameters, and a larger talent pool. The competition strengthened its reputation as one of Doha’s most credible dance platforms.

Super Dancer – Season 3

International Reach & Digital Integration

Season 3 introduced international judges through online evaluations, enabling global participation while maintaining fairness and transparency. This season set the foundation for Rhythm Rise’s international vision.

Super Singer — Doha, Qatar

Vocal Excellence Platform

Super Singer provided a professional stage for vocal talent across genres, including classical, semi-classical, film, and contemporary music. The competition emphasized voice quality, expression, and stage presence.

Voice of World

Global Online Singing Contest

Voice of World connected singers from multiple countries through a fully online competition format. It demonstrated the power of digital platforms in discovering global talent beyond geographical boundaries.

Cric Qatar

Community Cricket Platform

Cric Qatar is one of the largest cricket communities in the region, with over 900 registered teams. The platform organizes free weekly matches and fosters sportsmanship, community bonding, and youth engagement among expatriates.
