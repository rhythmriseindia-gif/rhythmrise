Rhythm Rise

Rules & Regulations

Rhythm Rise is a professional singing and dance talent platform designed to discover, nurture, and promote talent through a transparent and structured process. All participants are requested to read and understand the following rules carefully before registering.

1. Competition Structure

  • The competition is conducted in multiple stages.
  • Stage 1: Free Auditions
  • Stage 2: District Semi-Finals
  • Stage 3: District Finals
  • Further rounds may be conducted as required.

2. Audition Format (FREE – Short Format)

  • Dance (Solo / Group): Maximum 2 minutes
  • Singing: Maximum 1 minute 30 seconds
  • Auditions are designed to evaluate core talent, rhythm, expression, and stage presence.
  • Judges reserve the right to stop a performance once sufficient evaluation is completed.
  • In case of high participation, audition duration may be adjusted to ensure smooth conduct.
Auditions focus on identifying potential, not on perfection or elaborate presentation.

3. District Finals – Performance Duration

  • Solo Dance: Maximum 3 minutes
  • Classical Dance / Group Dance: Maximum 4 minutes
  • Singing: Maximum 3 minutes
  • Exceeding the time limit may result in deduction of marks.

4. Music & Song Selection

  • Allowed music genres: Cinematic, Western, Foreign Language, Classical, Folk.
  • Devotional songs are permitted only for Classical performances.
  • Songs containing vulgar, offensive, violent, or inappropriate content are strictly prohibited.

5. Genre Compliance

  • Western dance must use Western or foreign music.
  • Classical dance must use classical music only.
  • Folk performances must use folk music.
  • Incorrect genre selection may lead to loss of marks.

6. Group Dance Criteria

  • Minimum participants: 4
  • Maximum participants: 15

7. Props & Safety

  • Fire, sharp objects, weapons, or hazardous materials are strictly prohibited.
  • Religious idols, framed pictures, or sensitive symbols are not allowed.
  • All props are subject to venue safety rules and organizer approval.

8. Costumes

  • Auditions: Costumes are optional; decent attire is mandatory.
  • District Semifinals & Finals: Costumes are mandatory.
  • Indecent or culturally inappropriate costumes are not permitted.

9. Makeup

  • Auditions: Makeup is optional and does not affect scoring.
  • District Semifinals & Finals: Makeup is mandatory and contributes to presentation score.

10. Audio Tracks

  • Audio must be pre-edited to the specified duration.
  • Accepted format: MP3.
  • Audio must be carried on a USB drive (one track only) or shared digitally if instructed.
  • Late or incorrect audio submission may affect participation.

11. Selection & Advancement

  • Selection is based purely on judges’ evaluation.
  • Selected participants will be informed privately.
  • Participation in District Finals is optional.
  • Advanced rounds may involve a registration fee, communicated after selection.

12. Judging Criteria

  • Technique & control
  • Rhythm & timing
  • Expression & confidence
  • Stage presence
  • Overall impact

13. Schedule & Discipline

  • Time slots will not be changed.
  • Participants must report as per instructions.
  • Late arrival or absence may result in disqualification.

14. Photography & Media

  • Photography or videography in front of the stage is prohibited.
  • Rhythm Rise reserves the right to use photos and videos for promotional purposes.

15. Code of Conduct

  • All participants and accompanying persons must maintain discipline.
  • Any misconduct or illegal activity will result in immediate disqualification.

16. Final Authority

All decisions taken by the Rhythm Rise Management Committee are final and binding. Rhythm Rise reserves the right to amend rules, schedule, or format if required.
