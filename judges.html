Our International Jury

Rhythm Rise is proud to be judged by a distinguished panel of international singers, dancers, music educators, and industry professionals.

Priti Vikramnath

Ireland

Hindustani Classical Vocalist & Music Educator. Trained at Allahabad University with professional Bollywood training from Saregamapadhanisa Academy, Mumbai.

Judge at The Voice of World. Shared stage with Sonu Nigam, Benny Dayal, Vinod Rathod, Irfan and Shaan. Performed at Indian Film Festival Ireland with Javed Jaffrey.

Mohinder Jalandhary

India(Punjab)

Mohinder Jalandhary is a prominent Punjabi singer known for his powerful voice and vibrant stage presence. He has released more than 25 music video albums, showcasing his versatility and dedication to Punjabi music.

He has extensive experience in judging international events. Mohinder Jalandhary has performed alongside several Bollywood celebrities at high-profile events.

Jawed Bajwa

Qatar

Jawed Bajwa is a well-known singer from Doha, admired for his soulful renditions of Hindi songs. International performer and cultural ambassador with experience across multiple high-profile talent platforms.

He brings classic and contemporary Bollywood music to life with emotion and strong vocal depth.In recognition of his musical expertise, he also served as a judge for many musical shows.

Geeta Mahalakshmi

USA

Stage performance and vocal specialist with years of experience training singers and live performers in US.
