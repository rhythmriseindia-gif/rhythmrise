Our International Jury

Rhythm Rise is evaluated by a distinguished panel of international singers, dancers, music educators, and performance experts.

Priti Vikramnath

Ireland

Hindustani Classical Vocalist & Music Educator. Trained at Allahabad University with Bollywood training from Saregamapadhanisa Academy, Mumbai. Judge on international platforms and performer with Sonu Nigam, Benny Dayal, Vinod Rathod, Irfan and Shaan.

Sarath Kumar

Qatar

Sarath Kumar is a versatile dance instructor, choreographer known for shaping dynamic performers across the middle-east and India. He specializes in contemporary, hip hop, Western, classical, and Indian fusion styles. Sarath was also the official choreographer for Mrs. India Middle East – Season 3.

Mohinder Jalandhary

India (Punjab)

Prominent Punjabi singer with over 25 music video albums. Known for powerful vocals, international judging experience, and performances alongside Bollywood celebrities.

Jawed Bajwa

Qatar

Doha-based singer admired for soulful Hindi renditions. Cultural ambassador and experienced judge across international talent platforms.

Geeta Lakshmi

USA

Geeta Muppirala is a classically trained Carnatic vocalist and an accomplished Bollywood music instructor with a passion for nurturing talent across all age groups. With over 1,000+ hours of dedicated teaching experience, she has successfully guided students in building strong vocal foundations, musical confidence, and performance skills.

Bhavna Sagar Naik

Qatar

Bhavna Sagar Naik is a highly accomplished dance choreographer and dance fitness instructor with over 25 years of artistic and teaching experience. A professionally trained Odissi dance expert, she brings depth, rhythm, and expressive storytelling into every movement she creates.
