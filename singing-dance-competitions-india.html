Singing & Dance Competitions in India

Rhythm Rise is a premier singing and dance competition platform in India, created to identify, nurture, and celebrate genuine talent. Participants can take part in online and offline auditions judged by international jury members from India, Qatar, UK, Ireland, and the USA.
