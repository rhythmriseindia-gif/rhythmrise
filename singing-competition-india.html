Rhythm Rise proudly presents one of the most professional singing competitions in India, created to discover, support, and elevate genuine vocal talent across the country.

Whether you are a beginner or a trained vocalist, Rhythm Rise gives you a powerful platform to perform, get evaluated by expert judges, and move toward national and international recognition.

About Rhythm Rise Singing Competitions

Rhythm Rise organizes professionally managed singing competitions across India, offering both online and offline audition formats. Our judging panel includes international judges from India, Qatar, UK, Ireland, and the USA, ensuring unbiased and world-class evaluation.

Participants are evaluated on:

  • Voice quality
  • Sur (pitch) & rhythm
  • Expression & emotion
  • Stage confidence
  • Musicality & performance impact

Age Categories

  • Kids Singing Competition (5 – 12 years)
  • Junior Singing Competition (13 – 17 years)
  • Senior Singing Competition (18+ years)

Music Genres Accepted

  • Classical
  • Semi-Classical
  • Film / Bollywood

  • Folk
  • Western & Contemporary
