About Rhythm Rise

A Global Platform for Singing & Dance Talent

Rhythm Rise is a global talent initiative dedicated to discovering, nurturing, and celebrating excellence in singing and dance. Born as an initiative of Doha Music Lovers Group, based in Doha, Qatar, Rhythm Rise was created with a singular vision—to give passionate performers a professional, respectful, and inspiring platform to rise through rhythm.

Rooted in the belief that talent knows no boundaries, Rhythm Rise brings together artists from diverse cultures, backgrounds, and age groups. Through thoughtfully curated competitions, expert mentorship, and transparent judging processes, we aim to spotlight genuine talent while preserving the richness of artistic traditions.

At Rhythm Rise, music and movement are more than performances—they are stories, emotions, and identities. Every participant is encouraged to express themselves freely, confidently, and creatively, while being evaluated by experienced local and international judges who value authenticity and artistry.

We are committed to maintaining fairness, inclusivity, and professionalism at every stage—from auditions to finals—ensuring that every performer feels respected, motivated, and proud to be part of the Rhythm Rise journey.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized talent platform that empowers singers and dancers to showcase their abilities, gain confidence, and pursue artistic excellence—while fostering cultural harmony through the universal language of music and dance.

Our Mission

Provide a transparent, high-quality competition platform.
Identify and nurture emerging talent at all levels.
Promote cultural diversity and artistic integrity.
Connect performers with renowned judges and mentors.
Inspire future artists through fair recognition.

What Makes Rhythm Rise Different

Expert Judges

Local and international judges offering balanced and fair evaluation.

Global Reach

Online and offline participation expanding opportunities worldwide.

Artist-First

Respect, encouragement, and growth-focused feedback.

Professional Standards

Transparent criteria and stage-quality event management.

Our Roots

Rhythm Rise is proudly powered by the legacy of Doha Music Lovers Group, known for organizing successful cultural and musical events that unite communities and celebrate talent. Building on this strong foundation, Rhythm Rise expands the vision to a larger, international stage.
