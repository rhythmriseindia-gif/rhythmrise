About Rhythm Rise

A Global Platform for Singing & Dance Talent

Rhythm Rise is a global talent initiative dedicated to discovering, nurturing, and celebrating excellence in singing and dance. Born as an initiative of Doha Music Lovers Group, based in Doha, Qatar, Rhythm Rise was created to provide passionate performers with a professional and inspiring platform.

Rooted in the belief that talent knows no boundaries, Rhythm Rise brings together artists from diverse cultures and backgrounds. Through carefully curated competitions and transparent evaluation, we spotlight genuine talent while honoring artistic traditions.

At Rhythm Rise, music and movement are more than performances — they are expressions of identity, emotion, and creativity. Participants are encouraged to perform with confidence while being evaluated by experienced local and international judges.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized platform that empowers singers and dancers to showcase their abilities, gain confidence, and achieve artistic excellence.

Our Mission

Provide a transparent and professional competition platform
Identify and nurture emerging talent
Promote cultural diversity and artistic integrity
Connect performers with renowned judges and mentors
