About Rhythm Rise

Rhythm Rise is an international singing and dance competition platform created to discover, nurture and promote genuine talent.

Built on the legacy of Doha Music Lovers, Super Dancer, Super Singer and Voice of World, Rhythm Rise brings international standards of judging and performance to India.

With jury members from India, Qatar, UK and USA, performers receive transparent, professional and globally respected evaluation.

Vision

To become the world’s most respected talent platform.

Mission

To provide transparent competitions with global exposure.

What Makes Us Different

International jury, ethical judging and proven global platforms.

The Founder

Syed Rafi

Syed Rafi is a distinguished community leader and cultural entrepreneur based in Doha, Qatar, with a proven track record of building large-scale platforms across music, dance, sports, and social service.

He is the Founder & President of Doha Music Lovers, Humanity Cares, HIQ Group, and Cric Qatar, and also serves as Vice President of the Central Indian Association (CIA), Telugu Sports Association, and ICC Film Club.

Under Doha Music Lovers, he has successfully delivered internationally recognized talent platforms including Super Dancer (Season 1, 2 & 3), Super Singer, and the Voice of World online singing competition.

As the visionary behind Cric Qatar, he has built one of the region’s largest cricket ecosystems with over 900 registered teams and more than 15,000 active players.

Through Humanity Cares, he continues his mission of education, welfare, and community upliftment.

Rhythm Rise is the natural evolution of this global legacy — a professional, transparent and internationally credible talent platform.
