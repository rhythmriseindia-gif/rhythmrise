About Rhythm Rise

Rhythm Rise is an affiliated initiative of Doha Music Lovers, created to elevate genuine singing and dance talent on a professional stage. It follows transparent processes and structured judging standards while connecting performers with national and international experts.

The platform offers performers meaningful exposure, credibility, and opportunities for artistic growth with a global outlook.

Our Vision

To become a globally respected platform that empowers singers and dancers.

Our Mission

To provide transparent competitions and professional judging.

What Makes Us Different

Ethical conduct, hybrid judging and strong community foundation.
