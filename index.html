Our Talent Categories

Singing

Dance

Dancers of all forms are welcome at Rhythm Rise. Classical, semi-classical, folk, contemporary, western, and fusion styles are celebrated with a strong focus on technique, expression, creativity, musicality, and overall performance impact.
