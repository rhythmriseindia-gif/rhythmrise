Founder – Syed Rafi

Entrepreneur • Media Professional • Community Leader • Cultural Visionary

Syed Rafi has an extensive background spanning business, media, sports, and community-driven initiatives. In India, he has worked with leading organizations including Tata Retail Group, BK Group, and Titan Industries, gaining strong expertise in operations, branding, and large-scale organizational leadership.

Internationally, he has contributed to globally respected media organizations such as Al Jazeera TV Channel and BeIN Sports, gaining valuable exposure to international broadcast standards and multicultural audiences.

In Doha, he has served in honorary leadership roles including Vice President of the Film Club under the Indian Cultural Centre (ICC) and Vice President of the Central Indian Association (CIA), actively supporting cultural engagement and community development.

He is the Founder and President of Humanity Cares, a nonprofit charity initiative, and also the Founder and President of Doha Music Lovers Group. He runs Qatar Job Opportunities, a highly impactful employment-focused platform with over 130,000 followers.
