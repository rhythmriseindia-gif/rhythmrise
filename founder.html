Syed Rafi is a visionary entrepreneur, media professional, and community leader with an extensive background spanning business, media, sports, culture, and social service across India and the Middle East.

He began his professional journey in India with leading corporate organizations such as Tata Retail Group, BK Group, and Titan Industries, where he gained strong expertise in operations, branding, and large-scale organizational management.

Internationally, Syed Rafi contributed to globally respected media organizations including Al Jazeera TV Channel and BeIN Sports, gaining valuable exposure to international broadcast standards and multicultural audiences.

In Doha, Qatar, he held honorary leadership roles as Vice President of the Film Club under the Indian Cultural Centre (ICC) and Vice President of the Central Indian Association (CIA), actively supporting cultural engagement and community development.

A passionate sports organizer, Syed Rafi is the founder of CricQatar.com, a cricket community with over 900 registered teams, organizing free weekly matches that promote unity, sportsmanship, and healthy recreation among the Asian expatriate community.

His commitment to social impact is reflected through Humanity Cares, a nonprofit charitable initiative where he serves as Founder and President. He is also the Founder and President of Doha Music Lovers Group, a cultural platform celebrating musical talent and artistic collaboration.

He also manages Qatar Job Opportunities, a highly impactful employment-focused social media platform with over 130,000 followers, providing job alerts and career guidance to thousands of job seekers.
