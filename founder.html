About Syed Rafi

Syed Rafi has an extensive background in business and media, having worked with top organizations in India including Tata Group, BK Group and Titan Industries. Abroad, he has contributed to global companies including Jacky Electronics, Al Jazeera TV Channel and BeIN Sports.

In Doha, Syed held honorary positions such as Vice President of Film Club under Indian Cultural Centre and Vice President of the Central Indian Association (CIA). He is the founder of CricQatar.com — a cricket community with over 900 registered teams, organizing free weekly matches for the Asian expatriate community.

He is the founder and president of Humanity Cares — a nonprofit charity initiative — and also the founder and president of Doha Music Lovers Group. He runs a job portal social media platform called Qatar Job Opportunities with over 130,000 followers.
